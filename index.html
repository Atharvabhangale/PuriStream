about

Welcome to PuriStream, where innovation meets environmental protection. At PuriStream, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the landscape of pest management by offering safe and sustainable solutions to combat pesticide pollution.

In many regions, including India, the use of organophosphates in pesticides poses a significant threat to human health and the environment. These toxic chemicals linger in our ecosystems, contaminating water sources, soil, and ultimately finding their way into the food chain, endangering both wildlife and human populations.

Driven by a passion for environmental stewardship and public health, our team of dedicated scientists and researchers has developed a groundbreaking enzyme-based solution. This innovative technology targets and degrades organophosphates, rendering them harmless to both humans and the environment.

At PuriStream, our mission is clear: to provide effective pest management solutions without compromising safety or sustainability. By harnessing the power of enzymatic degradation, we are paving the way for a cleaner, greener future.

Process of Production

Making of powdered formulations of phosphotriesterase (PTE) enzymes

1. Enzyme Production: Microbial culturing: Bacterial strains engineered to produce PTEs are cultured in large bioreactors. Cell Disruption Centrifugation Initial Purification

2. Enzyme Extraction and Purification: Microbial cultures: Bacteria like Pseudomonas diminuta or recombinant E. coli strains engineered to produce specific PTEs can be cultured in large bioreactors.

3. Concentration and Drying: Precipitation Centrifugation Redissolving and Buffer Exchange Drying : Lyophilization (freeze drying).

4. Formulation Considerations: Additives: Stabilizing agents added might be included during the drying process. These can improve the stability and storage properties of the PTE powder.
